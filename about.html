About Me

Hi there - I am Grant Sadowski and this is my website that I created from the ground up using HTML, CSS and JavsScript. I got interested in coding in 2016 while living in Germany. I was looking for a creative outlet and I was curious about how websites were built and styled. But more specifically, I wanted to learn the design practices and trends around websites and how information can be the most effectively conveyed.

I learned about coding from a variety of sources online. I started with Treehouse tutorials and YouTube videos to learn the basics. Once I had a foundation, I started working on this site and adding to it as I learned new concepts and tools.

My goal is to combine my coding knowledge with my skills in Project Management and work for a software company.

Beyond coding, I have a few other interests that I like sharing and enjoying with others.

Running

I have been a consistent runner since college and I don't see myself stopping anytime soon. I ran my first marathon in 2016 in Muenster, Germany while I was living overseas. The challenge of training and completing such a race was such an incredible feeling that I hope to achieve again.

Since returning to Atlanta, Georgia I have been using my running time to explore the city around me and find interesting and unique things around me. I track my runs using Strava but I challenge myself to take a picture each run and post it on Twitter.
