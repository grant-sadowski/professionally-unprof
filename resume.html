Hi there. Thanks for stopping by.

I am an aspiring Web Designer and an experienced Project Manager from Atlanta, GA. I'm also an occasional photographer, traveler, and an obsessive runner, which pairs well with my foodie nature.

After graduating with a Bachelor's of Science degree in Mechanical Engineering from Georgia Tech, I took a R&D job designing wire feeders for welding machines. I realized that I wanted to work more on the business side of a company and transitioned to my current role as Project Manager. I was fortunate enough to spend 2 years living overseas in Germany, where I learned German and experienced an international working atmosphere.

I really enjoy project management because I get to interact with interesting people inside and outside of the company. I'd really like to work for a software company and learning to code is one way that I'm learning about the process of developing software.

Looking forward to hearing from you!
